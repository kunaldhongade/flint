# FLINT Finance Compliance Policy (V1)

## Objectives
- Ensure MiCA/SEC regulatory alignment.
- Verify cross-chain asset integrity.
- Prevent excessive volatility exposure in institutional portfolios.

## Rules
1. **Asset Verification**: Only recommend assets with a valid FDC-attested cross-chain state.
2. **Volatility Cap**: Reject any strategy where FTSO-derived 7-day volatility exceeds 25%.
3. **Audit Trail**: All trade recommendations must be bound to a TEE attestation with signed price data.
4. **Liquidity Check**: Strategies must have a minimum depth of $1M on Flare-native DEXs as verified by FDC.
